Tomas the Virtual Pet

Tomas the Virtual Pet is a Flutter-based mobile application where users care for a golden tiger as their virtual pet. The app features real-time mood-based animations and dynamic interactions that respond to the pet’s stats such as hunger, happiness, and energy. Players can feed the tiger using draggable food items, monitor its status, and watch it grow over time through an experience and leveling system. All data is synchronized using Firebase, allowing for persistent state management and seamless user experience across sessions.
